What is Jumpstart?

Jumpstart is a Magic: The Gathering format where players grab two themed 20-card booster packs, shuffle them together, and instantly have a ready-to-play 40-card deck — no deckbuilding required.

The idea is simple: each pack is built around a single theme (like "Goblins", "Angels", or "Pirates"). Mashing two themes together creates unexpected synergies and a unique game every time.

// THIS FORMAT

Instead of booster packs, each "locker" in Storage Wars holds a pre-built themed deck. Roll randomly to combine 2, 3, or 4 of them — shuffle together and duel. Skip the prep, start the game.
